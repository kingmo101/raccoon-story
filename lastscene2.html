Mysterious Noise in the Garage

Jeffrey Wong

After locking the door all you hear was him screaming and begging for you to open the door. After 5 minutes past the garage grew silent with no presence of your brother or the raccoon. So you open the door to find the grusome sight of the raccoon eating your dead brother on the floor.
